# Public Coffee Demo Source

When a brew tastes sour, most beginners assume they need hotter water or
different beans. The easier fix is usually a finer grind or a little more brew
time. When the cup tastes bitter and harsh, the opposite is often true. Keep
notes. If you change grind, water temperature, and dose all at once, you learn
nothing from the result.
