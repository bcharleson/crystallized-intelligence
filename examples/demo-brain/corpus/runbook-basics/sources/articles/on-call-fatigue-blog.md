# On-Call Fatigue and Documentation Debt

Teams often skip runbook updates because incidents feel unique. In practice,
most pages repeat the same failure modes. Without short, trusted runbooks,
every shift reinvents triage and burns out responders. The fix is boring:
small docs, reviewed quarterly, updated after every drill.
