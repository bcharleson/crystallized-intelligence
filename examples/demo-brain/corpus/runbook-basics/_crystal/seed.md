A runbook exists so someone under stress can restore service without improvising.
Lead with symptoms and the first safe action, not architecture. Every step must
be verifiable: expected output, rollback, and who to escalate to. Prefer checklists
over prose. If a step needs judgment, say what evidence to collect before deciding.
Update the runbook after every real incident — stale docs are worse than none.
