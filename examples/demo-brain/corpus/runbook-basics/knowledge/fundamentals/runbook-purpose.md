# What a Runbook Is For

Runbooks reduce mean time to restore by encoding practiced responses. They are
not wikis, not postmortems, and not architecture diagrams. A good runbook answers:
what broke, how do I confirm it, what is the safest fix, and when do I escalate.

Write for the person who just got paged and has ten minutes of context.
