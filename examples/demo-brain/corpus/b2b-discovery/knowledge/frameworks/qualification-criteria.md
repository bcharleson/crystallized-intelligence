# Qualification Criteria

Lightweight BANT for demo purposes. Score each call 0 (weak) or 1 (strong).

## Budget

- **Strong:** Budget range, existing line item, or named procurement step
- **Weak:** Refusal to discuss until "later" with no sponsor

## Authority

- **Strong:** Champion plus economic buyer or clear path to both
- **Weak:** Single contact, no intro offered, role does not own problem

## Need

- **Strong:** Specific pain in prospect's words, repeated unprompted
- **Weak:** Polite interest only; no workflow detail

## Timeline

- **Strong:** Event, quarter, or contract driving action
- **Weak:** "Someday" with no follow-up commitment

## Advance rules (tier 0)

| Score | Action |
|-------|--------|
| 3–4 strong | Schedule demo with stakeholders named |
| 2 strong | Second discovery or stakeholder map |
| 0–1 strong | Disqualify or dated nurture — do not demo |

This deliberately conflicts with some tier-3/4 sources that say "never
disqualify." **Trust tier 0 over lower-trust sources.**
