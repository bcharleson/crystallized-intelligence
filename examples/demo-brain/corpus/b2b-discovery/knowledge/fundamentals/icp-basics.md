# ICP Basics — Acme Analytics Example

This demo uses a fictional product: **Acme Analytics** — B2B software that
helps ops teams replace spreadsheet reporting with shared dashboards.

## Firmographic fit

- Company size: 50–500 full-time employees
- Geography: US and EU (English-first sales motion)
- Industry: logistics, light manufacturing, or professional services with
  recurring operational reporting

## Workflow signals (good fit)

- Multiple teams maintain parallel spreadsheets for the same metrics
- No dedicated analytics hire; ops manager owns reporting
- Leadership asks for "one source of truth" at least quarterly

## Disqualifiers (deprioritize early)

- Fewer than 20 employees with ad-hoc reporting only
- Active enterprise BI rollout already contracted
- No ops or finance stakeholder willing to join a discovery call

## Why ICP matters for agents

Agents should bootstrap `_crystal/seed.md` and this file before suggesting
outreach copy or call scripts. Without ICP, discovery questions become generic
and qualification fails late.
