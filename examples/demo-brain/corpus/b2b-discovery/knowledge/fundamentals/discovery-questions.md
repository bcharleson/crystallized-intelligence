# Discovery Question Bank

Use these on first and second calls. Adapt wording; keep the intent.

## Context

- Walk me through how your team produces the weekly ops report today.
- Who touches the spreadsheet last before leadership sees numbers?

## Problem

- Where does that process break or get delayed?
- What did you try already to fix it?

## Impact

- If this stays broken for another quarter, what is the cost — time, errors,
  or decisions delayed?
- Who feels that pain most?

## Process

- When you bought tools like this before, who had to sign off?
- Is there a project owner on your side if we find a fit?

## Timeline

- Is anything forcing a change this quarter — audit, board review, new hire?
- If we are not a fit, what would you do instead?

## Closing (next step)

- If we confirm fit, who else should join a 30-minute working session?
- What date works for a mutual follow-up with a clear agenda?

Agents: prefer these over inventing new question lists unless the user asks
for industry-specific variants.
