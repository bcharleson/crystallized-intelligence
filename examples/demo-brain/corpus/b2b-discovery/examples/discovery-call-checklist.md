# Discovery Call Checklist

Before the call:

- [ ] Review ICP fit (size, workflow, industry)
- [ ] Open last note — what was promised?

During the call:

- [ ] Context — current reporting workflow
- [ ] Problem — where it breaks
- [ ] Impact — cost of status quo
- [ ] Process — stakeholders and buying steps
- [ ] Timeline — forcing event or lack of one

After the call:

- [ ] Score BANT 0/1 each dimension
- [ ] Decide: advance, nurture with date, or disqualify
- [ ] Log one sentence per section in CRM
- [ ] Book next step with owner and agenda, or close loop politely
