# B2B Discovery — Principles

## Core Framework

Discovery is problem diagnosis before prescription. The rep's job on early calls
is to learn whether Acme-style accounts (50–500 FTE, spreadsheet-heavy ops,
no dedicated analytics hire) have urgent pain, measurable impact, and a path to
decision — not to force a demo.

## Qualification (lightweight BANT)

| Signal | Strong | Weak |
|--------|--------|------|
| Budget | Range discussed or procurement path named | "No budget until next year" with no sponsor |
| Authority | Champion + economic buyer identified | Single contact, role unclear |
| Need | Pain described in their words, repeated | Generic "we should be more data-driven" |
| Timeline | Date or event driving urgency | "Just exploring" with no next step |

**Rule:** Two or more weak signals after a full discovery call → polite
disqualify or nurture with a dated follow-up. Do not advance to demo by default.

## Discovery call flow

1. Context — role, team, current workflow (2–3 min)
2. Problem — what breaks, how often, who feels it
3. Impact — time, revenue, risk if unchanged
4. Process — who else cares, how buys like this get approved
5. Timeline — what happens if nothing changes in 90 days
6. Next step — mutual action with owner and date

## Key Rules

- Ask open questions; confirm back in the prospect's words.
- Never demo before problem and impact are explicit.
- Disqualify early when there is no pain, no path, and no timeline.
- Log calls with one sentence per section above.
- Prefer tier-0 playbook over tier-3/4 sources when they conflict.

## Common Mistakes

1. Pitching features before understanding workflow.
2. Treating "send me info" as qualified interest.
3. Keeping dead opportunities alive because "every lead matters."
4. Changing ICP mid-call to make a bad fit look good.

## Decision Tree

- Strong pain + weak timeline → nurture with trigger event, set review date.
- Weak pain + eager demo request → one more problem question, then decide.
- No authority + real pain → map stakeholders before next meeting.
- No budget + urgent pain → confirm sponsor and budget process, do not skip.
