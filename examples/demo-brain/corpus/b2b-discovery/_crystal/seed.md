B2B discovery succeeds when you confirm fit before you pitch. Start with a
clear ICP: company size, workflow pain, and buying context. On every call,
learn problem, impact, stakeholders, and timeline — in that order. Qualify
honestly: no acknowledged pain, no access to a decision path, and no plausible
timeline means deprioritize or exit. Tier-0 playbooks beat generic hustle advice.
One good discovery call beats ten vague outreach sequences.
